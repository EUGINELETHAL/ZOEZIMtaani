About Us

BUILDING A HEALTHY,DRUG FREE AND FIT SOCIETY.

more About Us

Zoezi is a kiswahili word meaning "fitness exercises" while Mtaani is a commonly used slang in Kenya meaning estate.Formed in 2016, Zoezi mtaani is an initiative that was started by a group of seasoned fitness instructors with their mission being eradicating the rising use of drugs in the city.Led by Silas Okuku,the initiative was launched at the sprawling Kaloleni estate.The founders had realised that many of the to Kenyan youth were getting hooked to drugs at an early age and thus it was necessary to engage them in fitness exercises which would help in the reduction of drugs.The program has grown from about four members to a community of about 500 active members

Studies show that almost 80% of the youth stay indoors scrolling through their phones, watching televisions and using their laptops and this impacts negatively on their health.This idle time makes the youth engage in drugs or other vices in the country.The zoezi mtaani initiative mission is to sensitize the and community on the importance of sports and fitness.Our team of experienced dedicated instructors work hard to provide an amazing experience for all. Participants are also provided with free counselling sessions if need be.
